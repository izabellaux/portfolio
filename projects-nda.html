Consulting

At Netguru, I consult various Startups, small- and middle-companies on how to deal with their design challenges and bring together user needs, business challenges, timelines, and budget. I lead in-depth design discussions with partners, customers, and dev teams. Working with small multidisciplinary teams and partnering closely with engineering, product, and business partners to find sophisticated but practical solutions to design challenges. I participate in sales demos to explain the product, share best practices, answer in-depth questions, and be a design advocate.

High Security

Four teams worked on this project. The business (based in London), the manufacturer (based in China), the development (30 developers based in Wrocław), the design team (remotely). As a primary designer, I communicated between the owner (visioner), design team, and developers. As a design team, we delivered mockups for mobile application, commercial website (RWD), and watch. I conducted workshops, brainstorming, explanation sessions. As a team, we used the following tools: Figma, Miro, Confluence, Teams, and Harvest.

Travel Swipe

In the team of two, I worked on the mobile application for US startup. The project was designed from complete scratch - research, benchmarking, defining personas, user journey, wireframes, branding, final mockups, and documented style guide.

Documentation portal

I worked as the leading designer (UX research, interaction design, sketching, wireframes, visual design, and development phases) to create a widely available portal for technical documentation, which is used every day by over 30 000 users. I worked in a team, with two graphical designers, who developed illustrations for the projects. Then, I designed two additional web applications for technical writers to empower search engine functionality by taking advantage of machine learning and DITA tags. Daily, I work remotely with three developers teams overseas (Agile Methodology). Today, my colleagues are using these products, which is a very cool part of our job as designers.

Solution for education

As a single designer, I worked in the project for UK client, where I designed a web solution for high-schools and universities based in United States. My task was to work on the concept, branding and user experience. 

E-commerce Solution

I conducted a User Experience, and visual audit for one of the biggest B2C e-commerce solution in Poland. Afterwards, in the team of two, I planned and conducted the workshop for the client to work on best patterns and challange current design decisions. After that I proposed redesign direction for the project.

Application For Services

For some time, I joined a long-term project, where I designed functionality for a tablet  application for service workers.
