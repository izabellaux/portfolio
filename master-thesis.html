Accesibility

Evaluating the modernity of the UI in the wake of accessibility. Developing accessible, fresh and new visual design, using Google Material Design components.

Analyze accessible literature, publications and materials in the studied area. Deliver the evaluated accessible website design, basing on literature and the conducted quantitative, qualitative and comparative researches.

W H Y ?

The idea of this research started with my own need. I am very interested in User Interface and User Experience design. In my previous paper named ‚ÄúInnovation in Modern User Interface Design. Conceptual Evolution, Technological Drivers, and Quality Factors.‚Äù, I investigates the theory as well as practical guidance on the modern UI design concept. My master thesis paper presents what the must-haves, performance attributes concepts and satisfies regarding the UI design are and suggests the UI quality measurement technique. It describes the concept of innovation in a modern UI design and presents the user interface quality measurement, with users‚Äô feedback as a unit of measurement.

Then, I was curious about why websites are, in general, either accessible or visually beautiful. Furthermore, after a brief investigation taken it came out, that not all accessible websites are accessible itself.

The want to introduce the process of the web accessibility evaluation and draw attention to techniques and approaches, that are best to focus on when enhancing a website with an available, and attractive user interface would help designers, developers and business owners to better evaluate their websites.

T H E S I S  S T A T E M E N T

In accordance with theoretical research, the thesis statement has been formulated. It declares that: since accessibility, at the present time, is perceived as a crucial aspect of UI and UX design, and most of the accessible websites are not seen as modern or beautifully designed. The effective tactic is to study the process of the web accessibility evaluation and analyze what tools, techniques and approaches are the best to focus on when we want to enhance our website with an available, and beautiful user interface, to create an accessible and modern environment for users to work with.

P A P E R   S U M M A R Y

The paper consists of three parts. The paper's first theoretical part presents an academic presentation of a user interface, user experience, usability, and accessibility concepts. It demonstrates tools and resources that can be used during accessibility evaluation. All the considerations have been performed based on literature research, articles, and valuable studies. This chapter also points out the importance of an accessible User Interface from the business point of view.

The second part consists of two chapters is focused on Razem Sprawniej12 portal evaluation in the accessibility and design aspects. The assessment is done using described validators and a checklist.

To begin with, the last part is focused on the proposition of the new design of the user interface layout. The result of actions taken in the first and second part of this paper became a basis for the new layout creation.
In the end, the work outlines the possibility of future research in the area of the subject of work. As user interface design is challenging. The author of this paper has to limit her focus to more visual rather than the technical aspects of the accessibility evaluation.

F U T U R E  C H A L L A N G E S

I had to limit my focus on the user interface and user experience design theory and concepts rather than the technical aspects of the interface development. The scope of this study was restricted to the one static proposition of new website design. Progress has been made in Razem sprawniej website development, but many important research questions and technical sustenance needs continue.

A validation puts emphasis more on visual and usable aspects than the source code of this web page evaluation. The future research should focus also more on technical aspects to make this website more accessible also for assistive technology. Those corrections could be connected with improving alternative texts, getting rid of redundancy and taking care of code hierarchy and transparency.

Besides visual and source code inspection, it is good to take this website under the screen readers examination. Assistive technologies are used by people with accessibilities, that is why the website should, as much as possible, suit to those kinds of assistants. Starting from preinstalled text to speech software to web text-to-speech converters. The good practice is to also evaluate this website using the Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT). VPAT is free of charge reporting format. It is developed to support consumers and vendors in recognizing accessibility features in products and services.
